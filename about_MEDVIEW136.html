About MEDVIEW136

MEDVIEW136 started out as a brainstorm project between 3 colleagues: Amy V., Dr. Frankenstein and Nerdy McNerdington.

While working together at their local hospital, they realized they experienced the same frustrations with most clinical software.

Clinical apps with confusing interfaces, vague software documentation that only a Jedi could understand, etc..

After several meetings with the software developers of their current patient dossier system, users still couldn't make their wishes clear.

Most developers were focused purely on technical solutions, as opposed to sympathizing with the doctors, nurses and administrative staff.

"We don't have time to babysit you whiny users!" was the usual response.

Our Goals For The Future

  • Department Based Workflow Documentation: custom-made manuals for your user group
  • User Experience Analysis: create a clearly defined list of improvements for your software
